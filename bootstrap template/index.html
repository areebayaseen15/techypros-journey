Steps To Getting Certified

Sign up for your Class

Start your SDI Open Water Scuba Diver eLearning course and master all the important academic information. You can complete this phase through self-study, at your own pace, in the convenience of your home or office.

GET STARTED NOW

Start your Pool Class

The next step will be to complete your in-water skill-development training. This takes place in a swimming pool or similar body of confined water, under the supervision of an SDI Instructor.

GET STARTED NOW

Sign up for Open Water Dives

The final step is to complete the required number of SDI Open Water training dives under the supervision of your SDI Instructor. Here you will apply what you have learned in real-world open water conditions.

GET STARTED NOW
